Different breeds of cats

Bengal cat has a leopard-like appearance which resembles their ancestry, and since they are energetic, much exercise and play is needed.

Persian cat has long hairs, round face and short muzzle. They were ranked as the fourth most popular cat breed in the world according to the Cat Fancier's Association.

Scottish Fold's ear are folded forward and down towards head, caused by a natural dominant-gene mutation which affects their body cartilage. As a result, this type of cats often need to be regularly checked for their health.
